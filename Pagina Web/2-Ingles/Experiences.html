Experiences

Los lavaderos de la reina, an essential place to visit if you live in Granada or are spending some time here. It offers us medium-high mountain landscapes that with the spring thaw make the site a fantastic place.

Great, unforgettable experience. 10/10

Incredible winter scenery with a blanket of snow. Recommended to go with the family and spend some time in the snow.

The route is a bit long, at the end it becomes a bit heavy and monotonous, if you don't like the mountains this is not the place for you.

Very nice place but we chose the wrong date, I recommend to go after the first weeks of spring if you don't want to freeze.
